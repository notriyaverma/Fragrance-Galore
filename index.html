Chypre: Sophisticated & Mysterious

Chypre fragrances are known for their elegant, complex blend of citrus top notes (like Bergamot), floral middles (such as Rose and Jasmine), and earthy bases (like Oakmoss and Patchouli). Often warm and rich, they may also include Labdanum or subtle animalic notes. Iconic examples include Chanel Coco Mademoiselle, Guerlain Mitsouko, and Givenchy Ysatis. ★

Floral - The Timeless Bloom

Floral fragrances are timeless and versatile, centered around flower-based notes like Rose, Jasmine, Tuberose, and Orange Blossom. Ranging from soft to rich or powdery, they can be feminine or unisex. Subtypes include Soliflores (single-flower) and Floral Bouquets (multi-flower blends). Popular examples: Dior J'adore, Gucci Bloom, and Chanel No. 5. ★
